About Me

My research focuses on bridging the reality gap in contact-rich physical interactions by proposing a unified data-driven abstraction framework. This approach decouples high-fidelity ground truth generation from runtime execution to resolve the fidelity-tractability trade-off.

Research Interests:

  • Physics-based Simulation
  • Soft Robotics & Deformable Body Simulation
  • Tactile Sensing
  • Contact Rich Manipulation
