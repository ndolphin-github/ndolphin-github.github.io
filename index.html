Welcome to My Research Portfolio

I am Taehwa Hong, a Ph.D. candidate at Seoul National University specializing in physics-based simulation and data generation for robotic learning.

Research Focus

My research addresses the fidelity-tractability trade-off in simulating complex physical interactions, particularly involving deformable structures and contact dynamics. I develop abstraction methodologies to create foundational simulation tools for training physically intelligent robots.
